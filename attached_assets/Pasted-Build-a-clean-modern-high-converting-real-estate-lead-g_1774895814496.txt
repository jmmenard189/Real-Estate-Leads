Build a clean, modern, high-converting real estate lead generation website for the domain mygtahomeseach.com. This is not a brokerage vanity site and not a personal branding site first. The primary goal is to generate buyer and seller leads in the Greater Toronto Area, especially for Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, Markham, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, North York, and Scarborough.

Use a professional, trustworthy, polished design that feels premium and fast. The site should be mobile-first, SEO-friendly, and built for conversion. The design should appeal to home buyers and sellers in the GTA. Make it feel established, local, and credible.

Tech requirements:
- Build in a way that works well inside Replit

- Make all text editable
- Include placeholder images that can easily be swapped out

Branding and compliance:
- Brokerage is RE/MAX West Realty

- Add placeholder compliance text where needed for brokerage review
- Do not scrape or display protected MLS/IDX data unless connected through an approved IDX provider
- Structure the site so IDX can be added later without rebuilding the site
